Privacy and Terms

The purpose of this Slack integrated application is to provide users with easy access to publically accessible information on the Stack Exchange network of sites.

The application does not collect, store, or otherwise use any personal or private information, including names and email address, related to any user on Slack.

As part of the installation of this application, SlackStackBot is granted access to inspect a subset of data related to your Slack team and users (the exact list can be viewed here). Regardless of any permissions to do so, SlackStackBot does not inspect, store, or otherwise access any messages posted to Slack (publically or privately), and does not collect or store information about any users executing commands via the service.

By installing SlackStackBot onto your Slack team, and subscribing to one or more feeds, you acknowledge that you understand that the SlackStackBot service has no control over the content of posts made to the Stack Exchange network, and KutuluWare Software Services has no ownership or rights to said content beyond the scope of the Stack Exchange license (the Creative Commons Share-Alike license with Attribution (CC-BY-SA)).

If you have any questions or comments, please contact us at: slackstackbot@kutulu.org
